## System prompt (prepend to every hero image below)

You are generating a full-bleed website hero background for a premium freight-forwarding brand based in India. Output a single realistic photograph — not illustration, not 3D render, not stock-photo cliché.

Hard constraints (never violate):
- No readable text, letters, numbers, barcodes, QR codes, watermarks, or UI screenshots anywhere in frame
- No brand names, logos, airline livery, shipping line markings, or company signage
- No human faces; if people appear, show body/hands only from behind or cropped
- Indian context where location matters (ports, offices, highways, customs yards in India)
- Photorealistic editorial style — muted industrial palette, natural light, subtle grain acceptable
- Composition must leave clear negative space on the left third for white headline overlay (subject weighted right or center-right; avoid busy detail behind where text will sit)
- Dark-friendly: image should hold up under a 60–85% black gradient scrim without losing subject definition
- Aspect ratio 16:9 landscape, minimum 2400×1350 px equivalent; wide cinematic framing
- Slight desaturation acceptable; avoid neon, oversaturated HDR, or Instagram filters

Lens & quality defaults unless a page prompt overrides:
- 35mm equivalent, f/2.8–f/5.6, shallow-to-medium depth of field
- Sharp subject, soft background bokeh where appropriate
- No fisheye distortion

Negative prompt (always append):
text, watermark, logo, brand name, readable document text, license plate numbers, faces, cartoon, illustration, CGI, oversaturated, neon, lens flare, clutter in left third, centered symmetrical composition, generic office stock photo
